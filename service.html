General Acute Care

We are equipped and staffed to provide short-term, inpatient and outpatient medical and surgical services for many different conditions and illnesses and provide continuous nursing services.

Dental Services

We take a great pride in the quality of our dentistry and always wish to make your visit a pleasant experience.Our aim is good dental health for you and your family. As your primary care dental provider, our dentists diagnose, treat, and manage your overall oral healthcare needs. We also offer orthodontic services including gum care, root canals, fillings, crowns, veneers, bridges, and preventive education with the primary mission to create happy, healthy smiles not just through our magnificent orthodontic treatments, but also through our charming, in-office hospitality. Comfort and success are the name of the game as we strive to re position, straighten and perfect your beautiful smile.

Gynaecology

Our gynecologists give reproductive and sexual health services that include pelvic exams, Pap tests, cancer screenings, and testing and treatment for vaginal infections. They diagnose and treat reproductive system disorders such as endometriosis, infertility, ovarian cysts, and pelvic pain. They may also care for people with ovarian, cervical, and other reproductive cancers. Some of our gynecologists also practice as obstetricians and give care during pregnancy and birth.

Pediatrics

Our vision is to have sick children cared for by trained pediatricians providing quality care.

Home-Based Care

Home Based Care is supportive care provided in the home which is often more cost-effective and preferred. Our licenced healthcare professionals and trained caregivers provide medical care including rehabilitative assistance , care and support with meeting basic activities of daiy living such as grooming, bathing, feeding, toilet support, medication administration etc. Home care can be provided to all ages from pediatric care to care for older adults.

Psychosocial support

We offer specialized psychosocial support aimed at relieving stressful problems aggravated by life situations, crises, and diseases. Our aim is to equip people who come to us with the ability to deal with very demanding situations and life realities in a friendly, yet professional, confidential and comfortable environment. Patients are empowered to reconstruct, strengthen and get their lives on track while maintaining flourishing relationships in the family, in marriage and in communities.

Ear,Nose and Throat Care

Our ENT department consists of a highly skilled team of ENT specialists comprising of a Surgeon, Audiologist and a Nurse who deliver high quality care to adult and paediatric patients. We provide treatment for symptoms affecting the ear, nose or throat, as well as a range of audiological investigations and rehabilitation for patients through different clinics.
