About us

Who we are

MyDoctor is a telemedicine solution aimed at providing professional virtual consultation at a patient’s convenience, with easy access to qualified doctors and nurses 24/7/365.Our services are an extension of the physical and walk in services provided by our collaborator hospitals across the country. You can also use our platform to consult a mental health expert or dentist.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide quality, fast and affordable telemedicine services to patients 24/7/365. with consistently high service level and utmost customer satisfaction

Vision

To become the pioneering and global leader in providing remote telemedicine services by constantly driving down the cost of healthcare and enhancing the patient experience through the application of advanced mobile phone and web-based telehealth solutions.
